Grievance Portal and Complaint Tracking System

At the time when everyone is emphasising on digital india , so much so that even complaining and tagging concerned ministry on twitter might solve your issue. As for otherwise you can simply start by writing an application to your dean or your vice chancellor. If it doesn't solve the issue you can further complain on UGC portal i.e. Home Page for your grievances under the students corner after getting registered yourself by filling in the required details.This portal is an online gateway where students can log into a website and post his/her complaint ...

Each department has a separate view of the Grievance portal where they can view the list of complaints. As soon as there is an update in status, the individual should be able to view it . A user can post his/her complaint along with photos/document to support their claim. He/she should also select the department the complaint should be assigned to. (Rather than user selecting the department, look into classification techniques to assign the department). After posting the complaint, the user can view the current status of the complaint.
